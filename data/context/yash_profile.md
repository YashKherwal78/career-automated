## HOW THIS RECRUITING PLATFORM STARTED
The recruiting platform was not originally supposed to become a product.
It started because I needed a job.

I noticed that job hunting involved the same repetitive process:
1. Find a company
2. Find a role
3. Check if it matches my background
4. Find a recruiter or hiring manager
5. Tailor resume
6. Write email
7. Track responses

The process felt repetitive and inefficient.
My first thought was:
"Why am I doing all of this manually?"

The original goal was not to build an AI platform.
The original goal was simply to automate my own job search.
Then I realized every step could become a system.

First it was email automation.
Then it became:
- Job discovery
- Resume selection
- Resume tailoring
- Contact enrichment
- Email generation
- CRM tracking
- Prioritization

Eventually it evolved into a Recruiting Intelligence Platform.
