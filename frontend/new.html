FOUNDER'S NOTE

We built the placement experience we wish everyone had.

I'm a final-year engineering student at IIT Roorkee. While preparing for placements, one thing became obvious: some incredibly talented students received countless interview opportunities simply because of where they studied.

Others — equally hardworking — spent months searching LinkedIn, Telegram groups, WhatsApp communities, and company career pages, hoping they hadn't missed anything.

Talent wasn't the difference. Access was.

CareerAutomated was built to change that — to help every ambitious student discover opportunities faster, stay organized, and compete with confidence.
